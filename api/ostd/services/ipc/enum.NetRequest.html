L2Send

Forward a raw L2 Ethernet frame to the NIC TX. data is the raw frame bytes (up to 1514 B, no virtio_net_hdr prefix). The Net Cell calls sys_net_tx(data) directly — smoltcp is bypassed.

Fields

§data: &'a [u8]
§

L2Recv

Poll for one inbound L2 frame from the NIC addressed to guest_mac. The Net Cell splits RX frames by dst MAC; returns Data(frame) or Ok (empty).
