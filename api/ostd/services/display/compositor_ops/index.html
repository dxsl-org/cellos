Opcodes for messages from cells to the compositor cell.

Constants§

ATTACH_GRANT
Attach an app-owned Grant buffer to a surface slot.
CREATE_SURFACE
Request a new surface of (w: u32, h: u32) pixels. Payload: [w: u32 LE, h: u32 LE] Reply: cap (u32 LE, zero-padded to 8 bytes)
DAMAGE_NOTIFY
Notify the compositor that a region of a Grant-backed surface is dirty.
DAMAGE_SURFACE
Mark a rect of a legacy (Owned) surface as damaged. Payload: [cap: u64, Rect: 16 bytes]
DESTROY_SURFACE
Destroy a surface and release its capability. Payload: [cap: u64] Reply: [0x00]
DETACH_GRANT
Detach the Grant from a surface slot before the app frees the Grant.
DUMP_FB
Dump raw framebuffer (debug only). Reply: pixel data of whole screen
GET_SCREEN_SIZE
Query screen dimensions. Payload: empty Reply: [w: u32, h: u32]
MOVE_SURFACE
Move a surface to a new screen position. Payload: [cap: u64, x: i32, y: i32]
RAISE_SURFACE
Raise a surface to the top of the z-order. Payload: [cap: u64]
WRITE_PIXELSDeprecated
Write pixels into a surface (DEPRECATED — use ATTACH_GRANT + DAMAGE_NOTIFY).
